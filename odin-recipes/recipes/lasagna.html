World Famous Lasagna Recipe!

Get prepared to make the best Lasagna you've ever tasted! This recipe is simple, easy to make, and best of all requires little to no cooking expertise. Expect to make another batch because the first one will disappear fast! Look below to get started!

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need:
  • For the meat sauce:
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 (24-ounce) jar of marinara sauce
    • 1 (15 ounce) can of diced tomatoes, undrained
    • 1 teaspon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the cheese mixture:
    • 1 (15-ounce) container of ricotta cheese
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the lasagna:
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (additional to the 2 cups used in cheese mixture)

Instructions:

  1. Prehead Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Make the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
    • Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until the onion is soft.
    • Stir in the marinara sauce, diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes
  4. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, and parsley (if using). Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Add a layer of meat sauce over the cheese mixture
    • Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce) twice more.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles and top with the remaining meat sauce.
    • Sprinkle the top with the additional 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
