World Famous Grilled Steak Recipe!

Get ready to make the best grilled steak you've ever tasted! This recipe is simple, easy to make, and best of all requires little to no cooking expertise. Expect to make another batch because the first one will disappear fast! Look below to get started!

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need:
  • 2 (12-ounce) ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Steaks: Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes. Pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Season the Steaks: Rub the steaks with olive oil. Season generously with salt and pepper on both sides. Rub minced garlic onto the steaks. Place a sprig of rosemary on each steak.
  3. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to high heat (450°F to 500°F).
  4. Grill the Steaks: Place the steaks on the grill. Grill for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust the time to your preferred doneness.
  5. Rest and Serve: Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes. Slice and serve.

Enjoy your delicious grilled steak!
